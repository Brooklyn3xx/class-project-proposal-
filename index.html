Application Overview

PhishGuard is a web-based application designed to help users identify and avoid phishing scams. From an end-user perspective, the application allows users to input suspicious emails or links and receive feedback on whether they may be harmful. The goal of PhishGuard is to improve user awareness and reduce the risk of cyberattacks.
